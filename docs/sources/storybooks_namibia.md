## About the Source

Storybooks Namibia is an educational website that promotes bilingualism and multilingualism in Namibia. It makes stories from the African Storybook available in Namibian languages including Otjiherero.

## License Details

All stories come from African Storybook and are **openly licensed**:
- Creative Commons Attribution 3.0 International
- Explicitly allows repurposing for other projects
- Requires attribution to original authors and African Storybook

## Attribution

## Content

- Children's stories for various reading levels
- Traditional animal fables and contemporary stories
- Themes: responsibility, gender equality, humor
- Translations by Otjiherero speakers
